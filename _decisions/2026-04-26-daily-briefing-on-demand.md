# Keep Daily Briefing Available On Demand

## Date

2026-04-26

## Context

The Second Brain is in its initial operating phase. The daily briefing routine is useful, but it should support focused work instead of becoming another mandatory ritual.

## Decision

Keep the daily briefing as an on-demand routine.

## Reasoning

An on-demand briefing gives current context when needed without forcing a daily habit before the system is stable. This matches the current goal of making briefing and end-session flows easy to run, while avoiding process overhead.

## What Changed

The Second Brain should treat daily briefing as a callable routine, not a required daily checklist item.

## Reversibility

Reversible. If a fixed daily cadence becomes useful later, this decision can be updated or replaced with a scheduled routine decision.
