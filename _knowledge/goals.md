# My Goals

## Short Term (next 30 days)

### Goal 1: Establish functional second brain routine
- **What success looks like:** Daily briefing and end-session are easy to run on demand, all projects documented with updated status
- **Metrics:** 21 complete briefing/end-session cycles, 100% of projects with defined next-steps
- **What's stopping me:** Missing personalization data and placeholder business context
- **Next concrete step:** Fill identity, tools, references, and decide whether the business module stays active

## Medium Term (next 6 months)

### Goal 1: Have complete portfolio of production projects
- **What success looks like:** 3-5 significant projects documented and deployed, visibility in dev community
- **Metrics:** 3 projects in production, 50+ GitHub stars, at least 1 client/partnership generated
- **What I need to do first:** Complete second brain setup, define project roadmap, start execution
- **Risks or blockers:** Procrastination on initial setup, lack of focus on specific projects

### Goal 2: Establish recurring income or fixed clients
- **What success looks like:** Have 2-3 recurring clients or predictable project income
- **Metrics:** Signed contracts, stable monthly income, active prospect pipeline
- **What I need to do first:** Complete portfolio, define positioning, start prospecting
- **Risks or blockers:** Competitive market, need for consistent networking

## Long Term (next 1 year)

### Goal 1: Achieve financial independence through tech
- **What success looks like:** $15-20k/month consistent income through projects, consulting, and products
- **Metrics:** Multiple income streams, recurring clients, established products
- **What needs to happen first:** Strong portfolio, active network, efficient delivery processes
- **Why this matters:** Freedom to choose projects and focus on real impact

### Goal 2: Build complete open-source second brain toolkit
- **What success looks like:** Kilo tools adopted by community, active contributions
- **Metrics:** 100+ active users, significant contributions, complete documentation
- **What needs to happen first:** Launch initial version, document use cases, build community
- **Why this matters:** Impact on how people organize knowledge and work

- Don't want to take clients below minimum rate that doesn't cover costs
- Don't want to depend on a single income source
